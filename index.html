The Superhuman Diet
Cut These Out Completely
- No Booze: Skip alcohol to drop empty calories.
- No Fried Stuff: Ditch fries, nuggets, anything deep-fried.
- No Sugary Drinks: Say bye to soda, energy drinks, sweet coffee/tea, or juices.
- No Junk Snacks: Avoid chips, cookies, or processed carbs like white bread.
- No Fast Food: Skip burgers, pizza, or tacos from drive-thrus.
- No Heavy Condiments: Pass on mayo, ketchup, or sugary sauces.
- No Processed Meats: Cut out bacon, sausages, or deli slices.
- No Sweet Treats: Ice cream, cakes, or brownies are out.
Load Up on These
- Lean Proteins: Grilled chicken, turkey, or tuna for muscle and fullness.
- Green Veggies: Kale, broccoli, or zucchini to fill up without the calories.
- Greek Yogurt: Plain, unsweetened for a creamy, protein-packed boost.
Enjoy These in Moderation
- Berries: Blueberries or strawberries for a low-sugar, antioxidant hit.
- Whole Grains: Quinoa, oats, or brown rice for steady energy.
- Healthy Fats: Avocado or small amounts of whole milk/cheese for balance.
